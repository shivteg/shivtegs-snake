How to Play

Basics

  • Control the snake to eat glowing red apples.
  • Each apple increases your score and makes your snake longer.
  • Avoid hitting the walls or your own tail.

Multiplayer Rules

  • Local 2-Player: Play on the same keyboard! Player 1 uses WASD, Player 2 uses Arrow Keys.
  • Online Match: Share the link with a friend on another device. Host controls the game state. If you crash into the other player, you lose!
  • If both players crash at the same frame, it's a draw. High score wins!
